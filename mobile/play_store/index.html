Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

MalGuard ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

MalGuard collects minimal information necessary to provide malware detection services:

  • File Hashes: When you scan files, we compute SHA-256 hashes locally on your device. Only the hash values are sent to our servers for comparison against our malware signature database.
  • Scan History: We store your scan history locally on your device and on our servers to provide you with a record of past scans.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected for analytics and troubleshooting purposes.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT upload your actual files to our servers
  • We do NOT collect personal identification information
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties for advertising

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide malware detection services by comparing file hashes against known malware signatures
  • To maintain scan history for your reference
  • To improve our malware signature database and detection accuracy
  • To provide technical support and troubleshooting

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All communications between the app and our servers use HTTPS encryption. File hashes are the only file-related data transmitted.

Data Retention

Scan history is retained for 90 days on our servers. You can clear your local scan history at any time through the app settings.

Third-Party Services

MalGuard may use third-party services for hosting and analytics. These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Children's Privacy

MalGuard is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
